Wholesale & Logistics

Transparent B2B policies designed to make bulk purchasing effortless.

Standard Order Process

  • Enquiry: Send us your requirements via WhatsApp or Contact Form.
  • Quotation: We will provide a detailed quote including estimated lead times.
  • Advance Payment: A standard 50% advance is required to begin production.
  • Production: Standard manufacturing lead time applies based on order size.
  • Dispatch: Balance 50% payment required prior to final dispatch.

Shipping & Logistics

  • Volumetric Weight: As flowers (especially 48"+ sizes) occupy large boxes but are lightweight, shipping is calculated volumetrically.
  • Calculation: Final shipping costs are calculated post-packing to ensure accuracy.
  • Transport Partners: We work with reliable PAN India logistics partners like VRL, TCI, and dedicated couriers.
  • Packaging: Specialized durable boxing is used to prevent any crushing or transit damage.

B2B Pricing Policies

  • Factory Direct: All listed prices are strictly wholesale/factory-direct. No middlemen.
  • Tax: Prices are exclusive of GST (unless explicitly quoted).
  • MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity): MOQs apply for standard wholesale pricing. Please enquire for specific product MOQs.
  • Samples: Paid samples are available for large upcoming bulk orders.
